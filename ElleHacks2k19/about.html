Who, What and Why

Why is this necessary?

As the graph on left shows, homelessness has increased significantly in the city in the past two years. Insitutions such as home shelters, churches, hospitals, community centers and more have been providing services to provide beds to get individuals off the streets. However, there is a need to organize intercommunication between institutions.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is centered around helping those individuals in Toronto who donâ€™t have a place to stay. We present a platform allowing those in need to easily access a database of information regarding surrounding shelters. Our goal is to provide a safe space for those in need, especially during extreme weather conditions. We wanted to create a platform to connect different institutions that already provide these services, along with other individuals willing to help, in order to provide easily accessible beds.

Who does this benefit?

  • Individuals in need of a bed
  • Hospital staffs
  • Housing shelter workers
  • Churches
  • Community members
